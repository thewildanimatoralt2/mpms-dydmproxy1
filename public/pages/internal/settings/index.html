Plugins

Plugins are features you can inject into Elysium to provide yourself with a better, more customized, experience. Plugins consist of mods, upgrades, features, etc.

Official Plugins

Official Plugins are verified by our development team and are made to be injected into Elysium. These ones have been listed down below for easier use without having to use our Elysium Plugin Docs guide.

Unofficial Plugins

Unofficial Plugins are NOT verified by our team and may have bugs, supportment issues, etc. Our developer team reviews these plugins regularly to keep your own personal deployments safe from nuking.

To find these Plugins you will have to go to our GitHub Plugins and our Elysium Plugin Docs page.

Elysium is a web based proxy made for users to play games, communicate with others, and generally have fun. Modding and community plugins help advance this more with even wilder ways to customize Elysium to the best of your experience.
